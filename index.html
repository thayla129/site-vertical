Victoria Secrets Romantic

The "Victoria's Secret Romantic" fragrance combines soft floral and fruity notes. It opens with fresh rose petals and red apple, followed by a heart of delicate peony, and finishes with a hint of musk for depth. The result is a light, feminine scent, perfect for everyday wear or special occasions.
